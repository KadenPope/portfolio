Photography Journey

  • 2016 - Inherited my Dad's Nikon D80, and loved the power it gave me
  • 2017 - Bought a Sony A6000, as my primary camera
  • 2020 - Still loving photography, playing with lighting & composition

Why Photography?

  • Emotional, mental and creative release - to help my brain deal with the daily challenges of life
  • Challenge to improve and develop new skills
  • Love being able to be a tourist in my own city, exploring and enjoying Vancouver

Gear & Software

  • Camera: Sony A6000
  • Lenses: 16-50mm and 55-210mm Sony lenses
  • Processing Software: Lightroom and Photoshop
  • Computer: Macbook Pro

Techniques

  • Going out with a 'theme' in mind, for example, 'red', 'shadows', 'emotions', 'light'
  • Don't be afraid to try new things
  • Pick something to photograph, and try to make 5 unique photos representing different aspects of your subject
